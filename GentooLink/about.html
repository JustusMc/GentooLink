Ken

Back-end Developer, Cyber Security Specialist & Founder

GentooLink was founded by Ken McGonigal on October 2018. GWS is a managed service provider which provides managed Information Technology services to its customers.

He fell in love with computers in 1984, back in the days of DOS and the Radio Shack TRS80. Ken started to raise a family in 1993 and Windows 3 was not very exciting so was involved in non-technical employment.

Ken's excitement for computers was reignited when he discovered Linux in 1999. Ken has been a fan ever since. Back in 1999 it was mostly nerds and geeks using Linux as their main operating system. Yes, Ken is a geek and proud of it.

Ken earned a Diploma in Computer Repair and Network Engineering in 2002. Ken can be characterized as a perpetual student, always learning and upgrading skill sets. Ken has had parallel careers: self-employed computer technologist and law enforcement officer.
